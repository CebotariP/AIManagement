Healthcare

Artifical Intelligence has been used for both predictive healthcare and preventative care (robots do work that would otherwise cause long-term health issues).

Businesses

Artifical Intelligence has been able to help businesses increase productivity, efficiency, and manufacturing. Business leaders see AI as an opportunity.

Economy

Artificial Intelligence can contribute to the overall global economic output. There are fears that AI can take away jobs, exploit, and abuse the economy.

Government

Artficial Intelligence can contribute personalized services and automated processes for the government. Some governments have begun to regulate AI.

People on AI

The general sentiment towards AI can be reflected through these quotes done by both rich and/or famous people, as well as by people who work directly with technology.

Elon Musk

Business magnate and investor, founder of SpaceX; CEO of Tesla, Inc.; CEO of Twitter

If there’s a super intelligent [AI] engaged in recursive self improvement, then it will have a very bad effect. It could just be something like getting rid of spam email. It could be like, ‘Well, the best way to get rid of spam is to get rid of humans. The source of all spam.’

Bill Gates

American business magnate and philanthropist, co-founder of Microsoft

AI is just the latest in technologies that allow us to produce a lot more goods and services with less labor. And overwhelmingly, over the last several hundred years, that has been great for society.

John Giannandrea

Senior Vice President of Engineering, Google

I just object to the hype and the sort of sound bites that some people have been making. I am definitely not worried about the AI apocalypse.

There is just a huge amount of unwarranted hype around AI right now. This leap into — somebody is going to build a superhuman intelligence and then there is going to be all these ethical issues — is unwarranted and borderline irresponsible because people who don’t understand the technology get very concerned rather than focusing on the positive effects.

Mark Zuckerburg

American business magnate and philanthropist, co-founder of Meta

I have pretty strong opinions on this. I am optimistic. I think you can build things and the world gets better. But with AI especially, I am really optimistic. And I think people who are naysayers and try to drum up these doomsday scenarios — I just, I don’t understand it. It’s really negative and in some ways I actually think it is pretty irresponsible.
